    It’s baaaack! Kylie Jenner is a certified hair chameleon, but it’s been a minute since we’ve seen the typically long-haired reality star rocking a cropped cut. Well, wait no more!

    The Keeping Up With the Kardashians star, 18, debuted an ultra-chic, chin-length bob haircut at the announcement of her SinfulColors and Charitybuzz Auction for Anti Bullying on Thursday, July 14. She modeled the fresh style straight and deeply parted on one side.

    PHOTOS: Every One of Kylie Jenner’s Colorful Hairstyles

    The Lip Kit queen documented the haircut via Snapchat. She captioned a video with her stylist at her locks with the words “bye hair.” She followed that up with about nine more short videos that showed off the perfectly angled cut.

    The transformation was dramatic, but this is hardly the first time Jenner has owned the flapper-inspired style. She worked a black bob at the Met Gala 2016 and at the Balmain for H&M launch in 2015. She has also tried colorful versions of the cut: a teal-dipped bob (in May 2014), a blonde bob (in February 2015), an aqua crop (in July 2015) and a blue bob with bangs (in August 2015), most of which were wigs.

    PHOTOS: How Kylie Jenner’s Face Has Changed

    Jenner, who was accompanied by her momager, Kris Jenner, on the blue carpet, wore a plunging Galvan jumpsuit in a pale periwinkle hue. She accessorized the ensemble with open-toe sandals, a Harry Kotlar diamond bracelet and diamond studs, and her eye-popping diamond ring on that finger.
