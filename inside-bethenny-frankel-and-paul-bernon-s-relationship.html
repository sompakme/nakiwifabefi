    28 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Bethenny Frankel is a fan favorite on the Real Housewives of New York and no one is over the fact that she left the show. There was never a boring moment as she had opinions and had no trouble letting others know how she felt.

    Bethenny shared that she's still friends with Luann and Dorinda which fans of the franchise definitely love hearing.

    It's great following Bethenny on Instagram as she shares fashionable outfits and selfies, and fans have noticed that has been part of a very positive relationship over the past few years. Let's take a look at Bethenny and Paul Bernon's romance.

    The Start Of The Love Story

    Now that Bethenny Frankel is no longer on RHONY, she has said that the show isn't inspiring. But although she isn't part of the Bravo franchise anymore, her love life has been interesting, and fans have enjoyed following along.

    Fans want to know more about Bethenny's current romance.

    Bethenny and Paul Bernon began their relationship in the fall of 2018.

    According to People, he is a movie producer and real estate developer. Fans of The Real Housewives of New York will recall Bethenny sharing that Dennis Shields, who she dated off and on, had passed away around that time.

    According to Reality Tea, the star gave more details of her dating history to Andy Cohen. She said that because she and Dennis were off and on again, she would date other people.  She said about Paul Bernon, "So last Christmas I went out on a date. I remember talking to Tinsley [Mortimer] and being like, ‘I really like him’ and I thought I was an idiot because I was saying like, ‘I don’t know. I feel like he could be like the one or something.'"

    Bethenny said that she liked Paul a lot but didn't feel she was "ready" for something serious. She was worried about it not working out and all the negative emotions that would come out of that. That is something that her single fans can relate to, for sure, as it can be tough to have a broken heart and to keep trying to find someone.

    The Break-Up And Getting Back Together

    It doesn't sound like the break-up was too bad, as Bethenny Frankel appeared on The Ellen DeGeneres Show and spilled a bit about what happened.

    According to People, she said, "You know, not everything works out and so many people have tried to define my life — our lives — with, 'When are you getting engaged?' 'When are you having a baby?' 'When are you getting married?' And I've just realized more in life, with people being more open and honest about their lives and what they want, that everything doesn't have to sort of end in a blue box and a bow."

    Bethenny also said, "We've had a great, great relationship and I'm really happy. Not everything has to end badly, some things just don't continue and it's not negative, it's just life."

    Bethenny In Love

    In early 2021, Bethenny Frankel shared she and Paul Bernon got back together.

    For Valentine's Day, Bethenny posted a photo of the couple and wrote, “Behind every woman… (you fill in the blanks here.) Happy Valentine’s Day xoxo #Loveyourself.”

    Bethenny and Paul are said to have been house hunting in Boston in the fall of 2019. According to Us Weekly, a source explained, “They live together in the Back Bay area [in Boston] now. She has been spending a lot more time up there. They are house-hunting in the New England area.”

    Bethenny was interviewed by Christene Barberich, the co-founder and global editor-in-chief of Refinery29 for a live UnStyled taping, and she said, “Real love, to me, is the sum being greater than its parts. You don’t have to like every cheesy movie, but [you need to find] a person who makes you want to be a better person. [Who] makes you want to be nice to them, love them, and take care of them – and vice versa."

    RELATED: Kate Chastain Revealed She Wouldn’t Want To Work With This Real Housewife

    After saying goodbye to the Real Housewives of New York, Bethenny Frankel told Hollywood Life that she felt more peace. She said,“I’m confident I made the right choices because I feel healthier, calmer, I have more balance in my life, and I have more quality time with people around me. It’s just not a frantic frenzy. It’s not the healthiest of experiences for many people, so this is a healthier lifestyle [now].”

    It definitely seems like Bethenny Frankel has found love and fans are happy that she has found Paul Bernon.
