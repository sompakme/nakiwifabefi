    He was born in 1956 in New Haven USA in a family of three children and he is the last born to the family. His grandparents were Russian who had relocated to the USA. He started his career in the in his early age where he became a co-writer to a song which hit the music industry. He developed his career later and started singing his own songs which have led him winning the music Grammy awards

    He has performed songs with other famous musicians like Celine Dion and has written songs for many others in the music industry. He has several albums which have continued to draw great audience worldwide.

    As per award winnings, Bolton has won 2 Grammys for Best Pop Male Vocal Performance (nominated four times), 6 American Music Awards, and a Star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.  As a songwriter, he has earned over 24 BMI & ASCAP Awards, including Songwriter of the Year, 9 Million-Air awards, and the Hitmakers Award from the Songwriters Hall of Fame.
