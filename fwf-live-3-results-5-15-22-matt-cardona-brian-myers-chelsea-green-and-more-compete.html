    FWF LIVE! 3 Results (5/15/22): Matt Cardona, Brian Myers, Chelsea Green And More Compete

    and Brian Myers held their third event under the Major Pod network banner, FWF Live! 3, on May 15. Cardona, Myers, Chelsea Green, Deonna Purrazzo, EFFY, and Trey Miguel, among other noteworthy names, competed at the show.

    27 Sep 2024, 00:00

    and Brian Myers held their third event under the Major Pod network banner, “FWF Live! 3“, on May 15.

    Cardona, Myers, Chelsea Green, Deonna Purrazzo, EFFY, and Trey Miguel, among other noteworthy names, competed at the show.

    The results are as follows:

    • The New F.B.I. (Little Guido and Johnny Clash) defeated Eric James and Jack Tomlinson; The Sandman comes out and gives a few fans some beer and saves The New F.B.I. from a post-match beatdown. He then gives Little Guido and Johnny Clash some beer and hugs them.
    • Brian Myers defeated Teddy Goodz; after the bell, Goodz cuts a promo and demands another match.
    • Slade defeated Teddy Goodz in roughly six seconds with a chokeslam; after the match, Goodz cuts a promo and says he won’t leave until he wins.
    • Maven defeats Teddy Goodz in about ten seconds by catching him with a dropkick; Goodz still won’t leave, so he gets another match.
    • The Big O defeated Teddy Goodz
    • Fatal 4-Pack for the FWF Interstate Championship: HEATH (c) defeated, Trey Miguel, Blake Christian, and Aaron Rourke
    • JGeorge Open Challenge: Nick Gage defeated JGeorge
    • Fatal 4-Way (Winners Face The Headbangers for the FWF Tag Team Championship): Shook Crew (Bryce Donovan & Bobby Orlando) defeated Stang & Steve Believe, CPA & Phillip Cardigan, and The Mane Event (Midas Black & Jay Lyon)
    • The Headbangers (Mosh and Thrasher) come to the ring and Mosh says that he’s not cleared to compete, but he has a friend who will replace him tonight; Scotty 2 Hotty comes to the ring.
    • FWF Tag Team Championship: Scotty 2 Hotty and Thrasher (c) defeated Shook Crew (Bryce Donovan & Bobby Orlando); Scotty 2 Hotty and several wrestlers do The Worm (or try to) after the match.
    • Deonna Purrazzo defeated Chelsea Green
    • FWF Hardcore Championship: Max Smashmaster (c) defeated Matt Cardona when Smashmaster spit green mist at Cardona, who then accidentally hit Chelsea Green with the title before Smashmaster pinned him with a Tombstone.
    • Effy defeated “Smart” Mark Sterling with a schoolboy pin; “Smart” Mark reveals that schoolboys are not allowed in the match, which is contested under “Mark Rules.” The bout restarts, and EFFY scores the win, even though “Smart” Mark keeps trying to bend the rules in his favor.
    • FWF Championship: VSK (c) defeated Dirty Dango to retain the title; he hit Dango with the title, and the referee fast-counted the pin. VSK and the referee hugged after the match. Colt Cabana came to the ring after the match and chased VSK out of it. He beat up the crooked official and announces that he’s challenging VSK for the title at FWF 4. Cabana and Dango dance to close out the show.
