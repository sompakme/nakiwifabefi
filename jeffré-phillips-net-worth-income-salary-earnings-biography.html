    Harvey Forbes Fierstein (June 6, 1954) is an actor, writer, and voice actor from the United States. He received the Tony Award for Best Actor in a Piece for his own play Torch Song Trilogy (about a homosexual drag artist on the search for real love and family) and the Tony Award for Best Actor in a Musical for his performance as Edna Turnblad in Hairspray. He also penned the book for the Tony Award-winning musical La Cage aux Folles, for which he received the Tony Award for Best Book of a Musical, and the Tony Award-winning musical Kinky Boots. In 2007, Fierstein was honoured by the American Theater Hall of Fame.
