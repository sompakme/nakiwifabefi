    If Taylor Swift was breathing a sigh of relief, it may have come too early, as it appears the singer is still being harassed by the stalker who tried to break into her New York City apartment.

    According to Page Six, the New York Police Department confirmed that authorities made another arrest outside of her condo, only a few days after they responded to calls about a suspicious man who claimed he was there to see Swift.

    Police Had To Arrest Taylor's Stalker A Second Time

    Though it took days for him to be arrested again, the alleged stalker (whose name hasn’t been publicly confirmed) was apparently outside of the apartment only hours after the initial arrest, which took place on the weekend, sources confirm to the outlet. It appears his behavior continued escalating until police apprehended him a second time.

    “By Monday, the alleged stalker was seen hanging around her place — yet again — at around 3 p.m. local time,” Page Six reported. “However, police did not take him into custody until 6 p.m.”

    They added, “A second eyewitness tells us that Swift’s security team allegedly asked police to arrest him away from the building.” Apparently, the man is now facing charges though they remain pending.

    It was only days ago that we reported Taylor had a scare when her apartment neighbors called police in response to the strange man’s erratic behavior. “Upon arrival, [police] were informed that the individual attempted to open a door to a building at the location,” a police representative said in a statement following the Saturday incident.

    Regrettably, Taylor Swift has had multiple encounters with individuals exhibiting alarming behavior. In 2020, Eric Swarbrick was arrested for sending threatening emails to her record label. Subsequently, in July 2022, Joshua Christian was apprehended after attempting to break into her Tribeca apartment.

    More recently, in May 2023, 36-year-old Mitchel Taebel faced arrest for trespassing at Swift's Nashville residence, marking another concerning incident in the singer's history with stalkers.

    Travis Kelce's Family Worries For Taylor's Safety

    With a years-long career that recently turned her into a billionaire, Taylor is no stranger to attention. But her personal life has been getting even more publicity since debuting her romance with Chiefs player Travis Kelce last fall. However, even his family has expressed concern over their safety, given how high-profile the relationship has become.

    For example, in an interview with NBC Sports in September, Jason Kelce, Travis' brother, acknowledged that their family has never experienced the level of attention it received since Taylor and Travis went public.

    "It's certainly been weird, the level that it is now," Jason, who’s also an NFL star, told the outlet."On one hand, I'm happy for my brother that he seems to be in a relationship that he's excited about, that he is genuine about. But there's another end of it where it's like, ‘Man, this is a lot.'"

    "[But it’s] another level of stardom that typically football players don't deal with,” Travis’ older brother explained. "So, on one hand, really, really happy for my brother and where he's at in his current situation with Taylor. But on the other hand, there's some alarms sometimes with how over-in-pursuit people can be."

    "Overall, he can deal with some of this," he went on. "As long as it's not becoming a threat to his safety and things like that."

    For now, Taylor hasn’t commented on her recent stalker situation.
