    Ice Cube has been rapping since the 80's, and fans have followed him since he joined a few groups and then went off on his own. He released ten albums between 1990 and 2018, and he also appeared in a few movies such as Boyz in the Hood in 1991 and 2002's Barbershop.

    Recently, fans think that Ice Cube should step away from Twitter and SNL even made fun of him. While his public reputation has definitely changed, one thing is for sure: he has a nice love story with his wife, Kimberly Woodruff, and fans want to hear how the couple met.

    The Story

    Ice Cube is sometimes in the news for interesting reasons, like the fact that he was sued by Mr. Rogers once. And fans want to know all about the relationship that he has with his wife.

    Ice Cube and his wife met when she was already dating someone. According to Cheat Sheet, it was 1998 and he could tell that they should be together.

    Ice Cube explained, “The first thing I thought I was, ‘Wow, she could be your woman.' The first time we met, she wasn’t feeling me, she was NOT having it. She did not dig me. I think she was in a relationship, so she wasn’t having it. But I ended up seeing her about six months later, and the next time, we had more time to get to know each other. She could feel my vibe and fell in love your with your boy!”

    According to news.amomama.com, the two met when Woodruff was a college student.

    The Proposal And Family Life

    Six months after they met each other, the couple was officially together and they tied the knot in 1992.

    Ice Cube proposed by buying a car and putting a ring inside it. According to The Daily Mail, he said, "I don't know if it was considered romantic. I had bought her a new car [a BMW 5-series] and when she went to sit in the car, there was a ring sitting on the seat."

    Ice Cube and Woodruff have five kids: according to Cheat Sheet, their kids are named Karima Jackson, Deja Jackson, Darrell Jackson, Shareef Jackson, and O'Shea Jackson Jr.

    RELATED: Ice Cube Blows The Whistle On Dirty Cops Called ‘The Executioners’

    Ice Cube's Marriage

    According to The Daily Mail, Ice Cube has described having a wonderful marriage. He said, "I like to hang out with my wife, talk about things, get some coffee, you know? Sometimes just understanding where the other person is coming from is helpful to getting along and being happy."

    Ice Cube told Urbo.com that it's all about "respect" for the two of them. He said, "It’s a true partnership. I respect my wife and she respects me. I still get the butterflies when I see her."
