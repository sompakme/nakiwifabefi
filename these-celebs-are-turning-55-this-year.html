    These Celebs Are Turning 55 This Year

    09 Jul 2024, 00:00

    It’s no secret that in the world of the rich and famous the older one is the less relevant they are — however, some celebs have managed to maintain in the spotlight even once they pass their 50th birthday. Today’s list takes a look at those stars who are turning 55 this year, and frankly — from Hollywood stars over models to athletes and even internationally successful chefs — this list is filled with A-listers who are not saying goodbye to the world of show business just yet! And why would they?

    Related: These Celebs Are Turning 35 This Year

    Keep on scrolling to find out exactly when stars such as Helena Bonham Carter, Patrick Dempsey, and Halle Berry — who certainly prove that age isn't that important — will turn 55!

    10 Helena Bonham Carter Turns 55 On May 26, 2021

    Kicking the list off is Hollywood star Helena Bonham Carter who will turn 55 on May 26, 2021. The English actress rose to fame in the 90s with roles in movies such as The Wings of the Dove, Mary Shelley's Frankenstein, and Fight Club. Since then, Helena Bonham Carter has been a part of plenty of famous blockbusters some of which are the Harry Potter series and Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street as well as the popular Netflix show The Crown.

    9 Adam Sandler Turns 55 On September 9, 2021

    Next on the list is Adam Sandler who rose to fame as a cast member on Saturday Night Live back in the '90s. Besides this, the star — who is currently very active on Instagram — also starred in numerous movies such as 50 First Dates, Grown Ups, Just Go with It, and Murder Mystery. On September 9 this year, Adam Sandler will turn 55.

    8 Salma Hayek Turns 55 On September 2, 2021

    Let’s move on to Mexican actress Salma Hayek who rose to international fame in the late 90s with roles in movies such as Desperado, From Dusk till Dawn, Wild Wild West, and Dogma.

    Related: These Celebs Are Turning 60 This Year

    Since then, Salma Hayek has become a staple in the movie industry and she has starred in blockbusters like Frida, and Grown Ups. The actress — who is definitely embracing her age even though she still looks much younger — will turn 55 on September 2, 2021.

    7 Gordon Ramsey Turns 55 On November 8, 2021

    Next on the list of celebs who are turning 55 this year is British chef Gordon Ramsay who rose to international fame by being a reality television star in shows such as Hell's Kitchen, Kitchen Nightmares, and MasterChef. Gordon Ramsay — who will turn 55 on November 8, 2021 — will surely remain one of the most influential chefs across the globe!

    6 Halle Berry Turns 55 On August 14, 2021

    Another actress that found her way onto today’s list is Halle Berry. The pretty and talented Hollywood star will turn 55 on August 14 — but there is certainly no doubt that she will continue starring in plenty of movies. Over the years Halle Berry — who currently has a $90 million fortune — has given great performances in plenty of blockbusters, some of which are the X-Men series, Die Another Day, and Catwoman.

    5 Patrick Dempsey Turned 55 On January 13, 2021

    Let’s move on to actor Patrick Dempsey who is best known for his portrayal of neurosurgeon Derek "McDreamy" Shepherd in Grey's Anatomy. Besides this, the actor — who has already turned 55 on January 13 — is also known for starring in movies such as Sweet Home Alabama, Enchanted, Valentine's Day, Transformers: Dark of the Moon, and Bridget Jones's Baby.

    4 Janet Jackson Turns 55 On May 16, 2021

    The first and only musician on today’s list is Janet Jackson who is the tenth and youngest child in the famous Jackson family.

    Related: 10 Celebs Who Are Turning 50 In 2021

    Janet Jackson rose to fame in the 80s and by the early 90s, she was already a huge international star alongside her late brother Michael Jackson. On May 16, 2021, the musician will celebrate her 55th birthday.

    3 David Schwimmer Turns 55 On November 2, 2021

    Next on the list is actor David Schwimmer who is best known for his portrayal of Ross Geller on the sitcom Friends. Besides this role, David Schwimmer is also known for starring in movies such as The Pallbearer, Kissing a Fool, Six Days, Seven Nights, Apt Pupil, and Picking Up the Pieces. David Schwimmer — who has certainly done a lot since being on Friends — will turn 55 on November 2, 2021.

    2 Cindy Crawford Turns 55 On February 20, 2021

    The only supermodel on today's list is Cindy Crawford who will turn 55 on February 20, 2021. The star rose to fame in the late '80s and by the end of the '90s, Cindy Crawford was considered one of the world's most successful models. Over the course of her career, Cindy Crawford has worked with plenty of famous brands such as Chanel, Versace, Dolce & Gabbana, Christian Dior, Calvin Klein, Ralph Lauren, and many more.

    1 Mike Tyson Turns 55 On June 30, 2021

    Wrapping the list up is former professional boxer Mike Tyson who rose to fame in time mid-80s. After his boxing career wrapped up, the star managed to stay in the spotlight and he even dabbled into acting with roles in movies such as The Hangover, Rocky Balboa, Scary Movie 4, China Salesman, and Grudge Match. On June 30, 2021, Mike will turn 55.
