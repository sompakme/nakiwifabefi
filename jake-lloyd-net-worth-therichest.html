    American actor Jake Lloyd has an estimated net worth of $3.9 million in 2013. He is known globally for his appearance on Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace as Anakin Skywalker. Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace is the first film of the Star Wars prequel trilogy.

    Jake Lloyd is also noted for his appearance on films such as Unhook the Stars, Jingle All the Way, Apollo 11, Hosts, and Madison. On TV, he appeared as Jimmy Sweet on ER as well as Ronny Collins on the third episode of The Pretender in its first season. He even appeared as young Angelo on the four seasons of The Pretender from 1996 through 1999.
