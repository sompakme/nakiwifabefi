    Spanish professional football player Juan Mata has an estimated net worth of $63.4 million.

    Juan Mata started his career with the Real Oviedo, to which he stayed with for three years. He eventually moved to Real Madrid youth academy, La Fabrica, and spent 8 years with the said club.

    He became pro when he was sent to Real Madrid Castilla in 2006-2007. After leaving the team due to a release clause, Mata signed up for Valencia CF and stayed with the team for the next four years.

    Mata transferred to Chelsea in 2011 after his four-year stint with the Valencia. He led the team in clinching the UEFA Champion League and UEFA Europa League title in 2011-12 and 2012-13 respectively. After his successful run with the Chelsea, he agreed to move to Manchester United in 2014.

    Juan Miguel Mata Garcia was born on April 28, 1988 in Ocon de Villafranca, Burgos, Spain.
