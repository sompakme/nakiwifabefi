    Along with sports; cars and women are the three most popular and important topics for men. So it is just natural that men will try to combine those three things together. That can only be done if one gets to drive a car that will prove to be a magnet for the ladies. If the car can provide speed and muscle that will give a sporty and manly look, then that will be the icing on the cake.

    Here now are the top 10 cars men drive to attract women.

    10. Nissan Leaf

    Show the woman you are trying to attract that you care for the environment. Your kind soul can help melt the lady’s heart. At only around $33,000, this is certainly not a bad investment. The Nissan Leaf is the first vehicle to fully run on electricity that is distributed on a massive scale by a major carmaker. This four-door vehicle can comfortably accommodate five people. Buyers can choose between two types, the SV and the SL.

    9. Mini Cooper Convertible

    Look cute and attract the ladies by driving a Mini Cooper convertible. This car costs only about $26,000. This car is very agile, easy to handle and fun to drive. It has retro good looks and is extremely fuel-efficient, meaning you will have more money to spend for your lady. Buyers can choose between the S and John Cooper Works model types.

    8. Land Rover Range Rover

    You can prove your versatility to your girl by driving a Land Rover Range Rover. This will only cost you a little above $75,000.  You can choose between two five-liter V8 engines with either 375 or 510 horsepower. These are actually the same engines used by the luxury Jaguar brand, but tweaked for the Range Rover. It has a six-speed automatic transmission and can get you from zero to 60 in just 5.9 seconds.

    7. Ford F-150 Raptor

    The Ford F-150 Raptor is a man’s vehicle. It exudes masculinity regardless of the angle you look at it. The top of the line model has a 6.2-liter V8 engine that can reach 411 horsepower. It is mated with a six-speed transmission and you can choose between a four-wheel and rear-wheel drive. It has tremendous capacities for towing and payload. Additional luxury features are also available to make it further appealing to women. It costs only $45,000.

    6. Jeep Wrangler

    Remember when John Wayne ruled the movie world? This may be the nearest vehicle that can come to a cowboy personality. If the weather is as gorgeous as your lady, then take the roof off and let her feel the wind touch her hair and face. For just $25,000, you will have a 3.8-liter V6 engine that can easily carry the lightweight chassis of the vehicle. The engine has a power of 202 horsepower and torque of 237 lb-ft. Plus, to show the lady you really know how to drive, the Jeep Wrangler comes with a six-speed manual transmission.

    5. Mercedes Benz S Class

    What better way to exude to a woman that you are already stable than with the Mercedes Benz S Class. Driving this vehicle implies class and confidence. The S Class’ top of the line model has a front engine layout with either a rear wheel or four wheel drive. It has a six-liter AMG V12 engine with power of up to 621 horsepower. The engine is mated to a seven-speed automatic transmission system.

    4. Volvo C70

    The Volvo C70 is a sporty vehicle that is the perfect embodiment of a young aggressive man slowly coming into his own. The vehicle’s roof is a hardtop that is retractable, yet fits snugly on the car. Its appearance is sharp both in the interior and exterior. It is a relatively simple vehicle that costs only $35,000 and it only has one engine and transmission available, yet, the car still offers the buyer a lot of options. With a 2.5-liter five-cylinder turbocharged engine, the car can produce up to 227 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated with a five-speed automatic transmission system. It is a sports car with the personality of a luxury car.

    3. Jaguar XK Convertible

    The Jaguar XK convertible can be described like the woman you adore – it is simple, sexy and sophisticated. You would think a car like this would cost in the six-figure vicinity, but it is actually available for only $67,000. You can choose between a coupe and convertible body type. They are actually almost the same except for the triple lined fabric roof of the convertible. The car has a five-liter V8 engine that has capacity for 385 horsepower and 380 lb-ft. A supercharged version is also available with 510 horsepower. Imagine impressing a girl with a need for speed by going from zero to 60 in less than 5.2 seconds.

    2. Bentley Mulsanne

    The Bentley Mulsanne is impressive if only for the price tag. At $285,000, that will surely attract the women who are after men who have made it big. This is the flagship model of the Bentley group. It has huge amounts of space for the interior and the trunk compartment. The car has a huge 6.8-liter V8 engine mated with an eight-speed automatic transmission. It has the power to create up to 505 horsepower and 752 lb-ft of torque. It is longer and wider than the previous versions. This car simply says that it is the boss.

    1. Maserati GranTurismo Convertible

    The Maserati GranTurismo convertible is sophistication and class all in one vehicle. Just the exotic sound of the name can surely make a lady swoon. The name Maserati simply flows smoothly out of one’s mouth. Whether you choose the base or the S trim, you will definitely feel the comfort and luxury of the vehicle. It is powered by a 4.7-liter aluminum V8 engine that has been derived from Ferrari. A Ferrari engine, now that is another way to attract the ladies.
