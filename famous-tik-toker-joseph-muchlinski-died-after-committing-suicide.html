    Joseph Muchlinski, a well-known Tiktok celebrity, unexpectedly passed away on August 21, 2023. The news of his passing was posted on social media on August 21, 2023.

    Joseph Muchlinski allegedly committed suicide; however, it is unclear why he attempted suicide. He was known as Van Viddy on Tiktok and became well-known for his TikTok imitation videos and produced engaging, instructive, and amusing content for his audience.

    His untimely death stunned the TikTok community, his friends, and others, which sparked a deluge of condolences and messages from followers and creators.

    Please keep his family, friends, and admirers in your prayers. His funeral and burial arrangements have not been made public.

    Through TikTok, VonViddy achieved popularity and a significant fanbase; he has over 100,000 followers across the app and over 20,000 followers on Instagram.

    According to reports and his last posted video on his TikTok page verified that Joseph Muchlinski was struggling with alcohol and drug addiction before his passing.

    He recently uploaded a heartbreaking, emotional TikTok video on his official TikTok account “VanViddy“, a day before his death.

    In the following video, VanViddy expressed his desire to be remembered for his good deeds, which appears to be a suicide note.
