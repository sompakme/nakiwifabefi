    03 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Over the years the Disney Channel has certainly been the starting platform for plenty of actors and musicians and today’s list is talking about the biggest ones from the 2000s. Everyone knows that Miley Cyrus, Selena Gomez, and Co. are still super successful and relevant but the question is which one is the most successful when it comes to their net worth?

    After leaving the Disney Channel, most of these stars have decided to pursue a career either in singing or acting (or both), but today's list is all about uncovering which ones have actually managed to become the wealthiest. Alright, now here they are — the 10 biggest Disney Channel stars of the 2000s, ranked by their net worth!

    RELATED: 10 Hilarious Dating 'Motivational Poster' Memes That Will Have You Cry-Laughing

    10 Ashley Tisdale — Net Worth $10 Million

    Kicking the list off at spot number 10 is actress Ashley Tisdale. As true fans of the High School Musical star remember, Ashley actually had her Disney Channel breakthrough as Maddie Fitzpatrick on the Disney Channel series The Suite Life of Zack and Cody in 2005. However, in 2006 she became even more popular as Sharpay Evans in the High School Musical movie franchise. According to Celebrity Net Worth, the former Disney Channel star currently has a net worth of $10 million.

    9 Vanessa Hudgens — Net Worth $14 Million

    Speaking of High School Musical – Ashley's co-star Vanessa Hudgens has also made it onto the list. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Vanessa Hudgens currently has a net worth of $14 million which puts her on spot nine off the list of the biggest Disney Channel stars of the 2000s ranked by wealth.

    Related: 10 Underrated Disney Channel Original Shows (And 5 So Overrated)

    Vanessa had her big breakthrough as Gabriela Montez in the High School Musical film series and while she is still committed to acting — over the years Vanessa has created some music as well.

    8 Shia LaBeouf — Net Worth $25 Million

    Number eight on the list goes to Hollywood star Shia LaBeouf. Many may not know this but the actor actually started his career as a Disney Channel star. Shia played Louis Stevens in the Disney Channel series Even Stevens which premiered in 2000, and after wrapping the series up in 2003 Shia went on to act in plenty of famous Hollywood blockbusters.

    According to Celebrity Net Worth, Shia LaBeouf is currently estimated to have a net worth of around $25 million.

    7 Zac Efron — Net Worth $25 Million

    Next on the list is yet another High School Musical star — this time we're talking about actor Zac Efron. As fans probably already know, Zac had his breakthrough in 2006 as Troy Bolton in High School Musical and ever since then his career only went uphill.

    Just like Shia LaBeouf, Zac also went on to star in plenty of Hollywood blockbusters, and according to Celebrity Net Worth, his is currently estimated to be around $25 million — which ties him with Shia LaBeouf on this list.

    6 Hilary Duff — Net Worth $25 Million

    Spot number six goes to Hilary Duff who is technically tied with Shia LaBeouf and Zac Efron as well. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Hillary’s is estimated to be around $25 million which definitely makes the actress one of the more successful former Disney Channel stars.

    Related: The Best (Non-Animated) Disney Channel TV Shows, Ranked

    Hillary became a teen idol in 2001 when she started playing the title character of the hit Disney Channel series Lizzie McGuire, and after wrapping that up Hilary went on to star in some other iconic teen movies from the 2000s. Currently, Hillary is playing one of the lead characters on the comedy-drama Younger.

    5 Demi Lovato — Net Worth $40 Million

    Opening up the top five of the richest Disney Channel stars of the 2000s is singer Demi Lovato.

    Yup, Demi may have started out at by playing Mitchie Torres in the Disney Channel musical film Camp Rock in 2008 but it’s safe to say that the actress has successfully transitioned to becoming a very talented and well-respected musician in the industry. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Demi Lovato is estimated to currently have a net worth of around $40 million.

    4 Raven-Symoné — Net Worth $55 Million

    Number four on the list of former Disney Channel stars of the 2000s ranked by their net worth is actress Raven-Symoné. While Raven has been acting in the 90s as well, she had her big breakthrough as a teenager when she was starring on the Disney Channel series That's So Raven.

    Related: Everything Zendaya Has Been Up To Since Disney Channel

    According to Celebrity Net Worth, Raven-Symoné is currently estimated to have a net worth of around $55 million. Just like most of the former Disney Channel start on this list who decided to pursue a career in acting — Raven-Symoné also went on to star in plenty of successful movies.

    3 Selena Gomez — Net Worth $75 Million

    Opening up the top three Disney Channel stars of the 2000s with the biggest net worth is singer Selena Gomez. As her fans already know, Selena started out on the Disney Channel by playing Alex Russo on the Emmy Award-winning series Wizards of Waverly Place in 2007.

    Once the series wrapped up, Selena continued to act but she slowly made the shift to music. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Selena Gomez is estimated to have a net worth of around $75 million.

    2 Jonas Brothers — Net Worth $150 Million

    The runner-up on the list is not a single celebrity but a band who had their international breakthrough on the Disney Channel. Yes, the Jonas Brothers may have been famous before starring in the Disney Channel Original Movie Camp Rock but there is no doubt that the movie took their fame to the next level. According to Celebrity Net Worth, the Jonas Brothers have an estimated net worth of around $150 million which means that Kevin, Joe, and Nick deserve spot number two.

    1 Miley Cyrus — Net Worth $160 Million

    Wrapping the list up at spot number one is singer Miley Cyrus. Who could forget that Miley started her career as Hannah Montana on the Disney Channel in 2006, and frankly, Miley has been a star ever since. Even during the show, it was evident that Miley is a very talented musician so it didn’t come as a surprise that her career shifted towards music once she wrapped up Hannah Montana in 2011. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Miley Cyrus is estimated to have a net worth of around $160 million.
