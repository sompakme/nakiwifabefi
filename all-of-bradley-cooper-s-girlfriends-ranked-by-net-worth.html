    Bradley Cooper was introduced to the film The Elephant Man at a young age and decided to pursue acting. He received a Master's in Fine Arts before enrolling in a drama school. Cooper’s television debut occurred with a minor role in the hit comedy-drama Sex & The City, which landed him multiple supporting acting roles. With Todd Phillips’ The Hangover, Cooper became a household name in 2009 and became a sought-after actor. Since then, he has starred in multiple projects, including The Silver Linings Playbook, American Sniper, and American Hustle, and made his directorial debut with A Star Is Born. His debut earned him three Oscar nominations and an award from the National Board of Review.

    Throughout his sprawling career, Bradley Cooper has garnered a $100 million fortune and continues actively working on projects. His charming looks and easygoing personality have also made him a crowd favorite, drawing media attention to his relationships from the past and present. Let’s look at the ladies Bradley Cooper has dated and their net worths.

    Huma Abedin ($4 million)

    The news of Bradley Cooper and Huma Abedin dating first surfaced in July 2022 after the couple had separately attended the MET Gala. According to People, Anna Wintour had introduced the pair to each other before their romance began. Huma Abedin is a political staffer who has been an aide to Hilary Clinton for many years. She started her political career after earning an internship at the White House in 1996.

    Dianna Agron ($4 million)

    Dianna Agron began her career as an actress in musical productions before earning her big break with MTV. She starred in minor roles for various projects before landing a notable supporting role in the hit musical drama Glee. She has continued her music and acting career since the show ended. When the news of Cooper dating Huma Abedin became public, reports of him having a casual relationship with Dianna Agron made the headlines. No official statement was released on when or if the couple was romantically linked.

    Jennifer Esposito ($6 million)

    In February 2006, Bradley Cooper began dating Jennifer Esposito, and within a few months, the couple got engaged and married in December of the same year in the South of France. But the marriage was short-lived as they filed for divorce in May 2007, as noted by Harper’s Bazaar. Esposito is an accomplished actress known for her notable roles in television shows such as Law & Order, NCIS, The Boys, and The Affair.

    Suki Waterhouse ($12 million)

    Suki Waterhouse is a former model who has starred as a supporting actress in various projects, including Love, Rosie, The Bad Batch, and Insurgent. She has also released one studio album, I Can’t Let Go, and an EP, Milk Teeth. The couple first met at the Elle Style Awards in 2013 and began dating. They parted ways two years later due to their 17-year age difference, as they were on different career paths.

    Olivia Wilde ($25 million)

    After Bradley Cooper ended his long-term romance with Renée Zellweger, he briefly dated Olivia Wilde in 2011, but the couple never publicly confirmed their relationship. Their romance lasted for not more than a few months. Wilde is a notable actor/director who gained popularity after starring in the hit medical drama House and made her directorial debut in 2019 with the coming-of-age comedy Booksmart.

    Irina Shayk ($25 million)

    Russian model Irina Shayk has an extensive portfolio walking the runway for some of the biggest fashion houses in the world. She also became the first Russian model to feature on a Sports Illustrated cover and has multiple endorsement deals. As mentioned by Elle, Cooper and Shayk began dating in 2015 and welcomed their daughter two years later. The couple parted ways in 2019 but remain close as they continue to raise their child.

    Zoe Saldaña ($35 million)

    Bradley Cooper and Zoe Saldaña began their romance in 2011 after meeting on The Words. The pair split after dating for four months and reunited, but things didn’t work out between the two. They have remained great friends since then. Saldaña is known for her appearances in science fiction films and has starred in the top 2 highest-grossing movies ever, Avatar and Avengers: Endgame.

    Renée Zellweger ($90 million)

    Oscar-winning actress Renée Zellweger is known for starring in romance and drama films. She became worldwide prominence after releasing the 2001 romantic comedy Bridget Jones's Diary opposite Colin Firth and Hugh Grant. She has also starred in musicals and won an Oscar for portraying Judy Garland. Zellweger and Cooper met while filming in 2006 but didn’t begin dating until 2009, as stated by InStyle. They mutually ended their romance in 2011.

    Other notable celebrities linked to Bradley Cooper include Jennifer Lopez, and Lady Gaga, with whom he had sizzling chemistry during their filming and press for A Star Is Born. Cooper enjoyed his single life for more than two years before his romance with Huma Abedin began.
