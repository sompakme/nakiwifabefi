    01 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Fresh off the success of her sophomore album Love And War, singer/ reality star Tamar Braxton and hubby Vincent Herbert stopped through the Breakfast Club to address mommyhood, her talk show The Real being picked up and being compared to her sister Toni. “I’m not even on her level, she’s an icon,” Tamar says.

    As for singer K. Michelle, she had some choice words for the fellow songstress. “I really don’t talk about the people who bully me, like if you do one good gesture, does that make up for you calling me a muppet and for telling everybody I lipsync? I don’t want to talk about her,” she says. “On my third day, I sold over 100,000 copies of my album, so we’re not even in the same conversation in life.”
