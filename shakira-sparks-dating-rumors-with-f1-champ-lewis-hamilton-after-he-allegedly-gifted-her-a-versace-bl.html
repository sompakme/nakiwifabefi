    Shakira has left her fans shocked and equally excited with the news of her alleged new romance with the seven-time Formula 1 world champion Lewis Hamilton. The rumors started spreading after the singer was spotted at the Spanish Grand Prix on 4 June 2023 in Barcelona. Both the stars have since neither confirmed nor denied the rumors.

    Shakira was in Barcelona to drop her kids to her ex-husband and footballer Gerard Piqué's house when Lewis Hamilton allegedly invited her to the race. The singer was then spotted in the Mercedes garage in the paddock, the team for which Lewis drives. She also took to Instagram, saying it was "Nice to be back in Barcelona," along with a picture of her on the trackside.

    However, this wasn't the first time the singer attended a race. She was also present at the Miami Grand Prix, rooting for Hamilton from a yacht. Post the race, the two allegedly went sailing together with a bunch of their friends. They were also seen getting cozy at a dinner after the Spanish Grand Prix. The recent events have led to fans speculating there's romance broiling between the two.

    Hamilton Seems To Be All In

    Lewis Hamilton seems to be getting the feels for Shakira as he wanted her to be there on the sidelines while he gave his best performance of the year so far. The Mercedes driver has been struggling with his car all season, until the race in Barcelona. He seemed to have gained some serious speed as he finished in second place, his best race finish in the season so far. The driver's fans have given credit for this result to the Colombian singer.

    According to speculations, Lewis wooed Shakira with an expensive Versace blouse as an invitation to the race. A week before the race, the driver joked about wanting to find a Latina girlfriend, which further solidifies their dating rumors.

    After Hamilton's outstanding race, the two were seen partying together along with other sports stars including Neymar Jr. and Kylian Mbappe. A few sources also claim that Lewis had his arm around the singer's waist as they cozied up.

    Shakira Might Have Other Suitors

    Lewis Hamilton may not be the only one trying to get Shakira's attention. During the Miami Grand Prix, Tom Cruise was also present at the race and was allegedly trying to flirt with Shakira. Sources claimed that the singer had to beg Tom Cruise to stop hitting on her.

    She was also linked up with the Miami Heat player Jimmy Butler. Although the rumors died down, fans are shipping her alleged romance with the seven-time Formula 1 world champion. The Colombian singer seems to have found someone suitable a year after her split from her husband Gerard Piqué.

    On the flip side, Lewis has been out of the dating game for a long time. One of his teammates claimed that the driver has "been single for far too long now" and needs to get back into the dating world.
