    Lil Scrappy Divorce: Rumors &amp; Truth Unfold In Love &amp; Hip Hop Atlanta

    The new season of Love And Hip Hop Atlanta is bound to be full of drama, especially with the recent news of Lil Scrappy divorce. However, its not just Momma Dees meddling that caused the couples split.

    25 Aug 2024, 00:00

    The new season of “Love And Hip Hop Atlanta” is bound to be full of drama, especially with the recent news of Lil Scrappy divorce. 

    However, it’s not just Momma Dee’s meddling that caused the couple’s split.

    In fact, Lil Scrappy’s ex-girlfriend might have been the breaking point.

    In a trailer released by MTV, Scrappy, and Bambi are seen discussing the state of their marriage.

    Feeling the tension, Scrappy puts the blame on Bambi, saying, “You got a family or whatever but you don’t want that family.”

    However, his words only opened the door for Bambi to reveal his betrayal. “You were just in a person’s DMs that you used to date,” she said. 

    This prompted rumors about Scrappy’s communication with his ex-girlfriend, Diamond.

    The status of Scrappy and Bambi’s relationship in 2023

    According to sources, Lil Scrappy and Adizia “Bambi” Benson, known from “Love and Hip Hop: Atlanta,” have apparently decided to end their five-year marriage and part ways after having three children together.

    Lil Scrappy gained fame in the early 2000s following the release of his debut album “Bred 2 Die Born 2 Live” and his hit single “Money in the Bank.” 

    He has since released several other albums and appeared in numerous reality TV shows, including “Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta.”

    Diamond’s presence

    The trailer also shows Scrappy and Diamond face to face, further fueling speculation about their relationship. 

    Momma Dee later tells someone that Diamond could be pregnant by Scrappy, saying, “Bout a week ago she said she was waiting on her period.”

    While there is no confirmation of a pregnancy, the presence of Diamond in the new season of LHHATL indicates a deeper involvement in Lil Scrappy’s life, possibly causing a strain on his relationship with Bambi.

    Bambi’s confession

    Despite the rumors, Bambi confirms that her marriage with Scrappy is officially over. “We are officially going through a divorce,” she reveals.

    In response, Erica Mena weighs in and accuses Bambi of not fulfilling her duties as a wife. “She never did no wife **!” Erica argues.

    Erica Dixon’s involvement in Lil Scrappy divorce

    But Bambi’s problems with Lil Scrappy’s family don’t end there. Erica Dixon, Scrappy’s baby momma, also adds fuel to the fire. 

    In the trailer, Bambi and Erica have a heated conversation, where Erica admits that her co-parenting relationship with Scrappy and Bambi is “pretty much nonexistent.”

    It’s clear that Erica Dixon’s presence continues to cause tension, despite previous good terms with Bambi.

    Erica even tries to run up on Bambi, indicating serious issues between them.

    Other explosive moments

    Bambi’s conflict with Erica Dixon is not the only drama on the new season of LHHATL. 

    Erica Mena and Spice also get into a heated argument over Spice’s close relationship with Amara La Negra.

    With the addition of newcomer Erica Banks and model Jessica White, the new season is setting up for explosive moments that fans won’t want to miss.

    Final thoughts on Lil Scrappy divorce

    While his ex-girlfriend may not have entirely caused Lil Scrappy and Bambi’s split, her presence in the new season of “Love And Hip Hop Atlanta” suggests that she did play a role in their relationship struggles.

    With tensions high between Bambi and Lil Scrappy’s family and other explosive moments between cast members, the new season is sure to be a rollercoaster ride for fans of the show.
