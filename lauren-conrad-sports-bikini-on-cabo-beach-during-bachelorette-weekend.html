    Such a beach babe! Lauren Conrad posed in the sand in a bikini while on her bachelorette trip in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico this past weekend. 

    The businesswoman and author, 28, slipped into a two-piece ahead of her upcoming wedding to fiancé William Tell. Conrad left the States to celebrate her bachelorette with a group of friends, including her Laguna Beach/The Hills costar and longtime bestie Lo Bosworth. 

    The Hills alum shared a swimsuit shot via Instagram, showing herself, Bosworth, and pal Jeannie Scharetg playing in the ocean. Conrad posted the photo on Sunday evening, June 22, at the end of her Mexican getaway. 

    “Missing this beach already!” she captioned the cute picture. 

    The girls’ weekend in Cabo included days at the beach and nights out on the town. Conrad, who is set to marry musician and recent law school graduate Tell in the coming months, was also snapped by paparazzi while in the black string bikini.
