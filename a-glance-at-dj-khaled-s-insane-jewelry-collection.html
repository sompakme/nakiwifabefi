    Khaled Mohammed Khaled, better known as DJ Khaled, began his career as a DJ and radio host before releasing music as a part of a hip-hop band called Terror Squad. Now a successful record producer, executive, and hip-hop sensation, Khaled has managed to release chart-topping music by collaborating with A-list artists in Hollywood. He has amassed a $75 million net worth through his music, sales, and wise investments in lucrative businesses. Khaled never hesitates to promote his brands and show off his wealth on social media to his 27.3 million followers. He primarily shows off his jewelry collection that features rare and valuable pieces unlike anything seen before.

    From expensive chains that shimmer with diamonds to important jewels gifted by friends that hold a sentimental value, DJ Khaled preserves things that matter to him. Each more beautiful than the other, let’s take a look at DJ Khaled’s insane jewelry collection.

    Roc-A-Fella Cuban Chain

    One of the most significant and priceless jewelry pieces in his collection is a Roc-A-Fella Cuban chain that he received from Jay-Z. Not just a chain but the last Roc-A-Fella chain that has been gifted before to rappers like Kanye West and Meek Mill. According to BET, made with encrusted diamonds and yellow gold, the pendant is inspired by the original Roc-A-Fella logo in 1996 of the record label founded by Jay-Z.

    ‘ASAHD’ Rope Chain

    DJ Khaled is a doting father, and he loves to wear his children’s names as pendants proudly. He owns a rope chain with the words Asahd encrusted in diamonds. He made a similar chain smaller for his son with the words Father of Asahd encrusted in 14-carat VS diamonds worth $15,000.

    RELATED: Green Glimmer: 10 Most Expensive Chains Owned By Rappers

    Owl And Key Chain

    Gifted by his dear friend Drake, the Owl and Key chain features an ice-out OVO owl mascot, the logo of Drake’s company, along with a key and lion referenced to the catchphrases used by DJ Khaled, as stated by Complex. Drake was seen wearing a similar chain in a video produced by Khaled that features Justin Bieber.

    Allah Diamond Chain

    A true believer in the faith of God, DJ Khaled does not hide his faith from the world as he is spotted wearing an Allah-encrusted diamond chain around his neck every day. A religious person, the rapper received a lot of traction when he first debuted the pendant. However, he still wears it as an everyday accessory and his other jewels.

    We The Best Logo Pendant

    This jewelry piece not only represents a brand but the hard work DJ Khaled has put into creating a successful record label called We The Best. The chain has a diamond, and yellow gold encrusted We The Best logo that Khaled is often sporting when promoting his company or the artists who work for him. He usually posts the chain’s snap on social media to motivate others to be their best self.

    Pristine Diamond Baguettes

    Designed by Pristine Jewelers, DJ Khaled purchased an elegant and expensive chain that features more than twenty diamond baguettes that costs a few hundred thousand dollars. The rapper accessorized the rest of his look and purchased a matching bracelet to match it with the chain.

    Patek Philippe Nautilus Encrusted With Diamond Baguettes

    A watch that costs more than a home, DJ Khaled splashed out a whopping $300,000 on a custom Patek Philippe watch that is encrusted with diamond baguettes, as noted by GQ. The rare timepiece was created to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the Nautilus lineup and is covered in diamonds from every direction.

    Rolex Day-Date Platinum With Arabic Numerals

    Costing $117,500, the Rolex Day-Date Platinum watch was custom-made with Arabic numerals to commemorate his Arabic roots. The hour markers have Arabic hour numbers along with a 40 mm case that looks unique on the wrist. Khaled had the watch customized with diamonds from Pristine Jewelers.

    Rolex President Day-Date Gold Diamond Watch

    While the watch was once seen on the wrists of only Presidents and diplomats, the Rolex President Day-Date watch in yellow gold has become a popular choice for artists and athletes. Khaled owns two timepieces made from 18-carat gold, and the bezel and dial are manufactured in the original Rolex factory while the diamond bracelet remains customized.

    Pristine Diamond-Encrusted Watch

    In November 2021, DJ Khaled added another diamond watch to his collection when his friend Drake gifted him an ice-out Diamond-Encrusted watch designed by Pristine Jewelers, as reported by Hype Beast. The 18-carat diamond watch is set with 97-carat worth of emerald-cut diamonds and costs a whopping $450,000.

    From custom pendants he received as gifts to splurging on blingy watches that outshine everything else in the room, DJ Khaled knows how to wear the best jewelry for every occasion. The rapper has an ever-growing collection of jewels, and he showers similar love on his children, buying them miniature pieces similar to his collection. The recent addition to his collection is a pendant and watch set from Jacob And Co. that he purchased in January 2022.
