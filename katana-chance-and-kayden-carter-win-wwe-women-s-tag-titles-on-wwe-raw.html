    The WWE Women’s Tag Team Championships have changed hands.

    On the December 18 episode of WWE RAW, Katana Chance and Kayden Carter defeated Chelsea Green and Piper Niven to win the WWE Women’s Tag Team Championships. In doing so, they won their first titles since they were moved to RAW in the 2023 WWE Draft.

    Carter and Chance are former NXT Women’s Tag Team Champions. They previously won the titles in August 2022 and lost them at NXT Vengeance Day in February.

    Green and her former partner Sonya Deville won the WWE Women’s Tag Team Champions by defeating Liv Morgan and Raquel Rodriguez on the July 17 episode of WWE RAW. Deville went down with a torn ACL. Niven was revealed as Green’s new partner on the August 14 episode of RAW.
