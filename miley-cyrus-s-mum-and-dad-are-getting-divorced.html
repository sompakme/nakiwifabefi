    They share five children together including singer and actress Miley and her sister Noah.

    Tish Files For Divorce After 30 Years Together

    Court documents acquired by TMZ state that Tish, 54, filed for a separation from the musician Billy, 60, in Tennessee last week.

    According to court records, Tish filed docs in Tennessee last week. Their youngest child, Noah, is 22, so there will be no custody case involved. In the obtained divorce papers, Tish says the couple hasn't lived together for more than 2 years ... and lists "irreconcilable differences" as the reason for the split. She's asking for an equal distribution of all marital assets.

Billy Ray filed for divorce in 2010, but changed his mind soon after. In 2013, she filed for divorce, but she backed down from the idea within months.

    Tish and Billy Ray married in 1993 in their living room in Franklin, Tennessee. Miley followed in their footsteps, marrying actor Liam Hemsworth in the same spot in 2018. Cyrus and Hemsworth finalized their divorce in January 2020.

    Couple Not Seen Together Since 2020

    Fans noticed Billy wasn't around the Cyrus family for the holidays. He did not appear in any photos and videos posted by Miley, leading some people to wonder if there was a fracture in their family. The last time we saw Tish and Billy together publicly was in 2020.

    Together Tish and Billy have five kids: Miley, 29, Noah, 22, Trace, 33, Braison, 27, and Brandi, 34.
