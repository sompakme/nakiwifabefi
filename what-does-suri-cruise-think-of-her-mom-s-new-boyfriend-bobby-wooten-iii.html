    Who Is Katie Holmes's New Boyfriend?

    Bobby Wooten III is a musician and producer who was born in New Jersey and raised in Chicago. He spent his life creating music and has racked up quite the resume. He's worked on Broadway where his play was nominated for a Grammy, as well as performed at Coachella alongside Carly Rae Jepsen. He's most notable for being a bass player.

    He met Katie Holmes when a mutual friend of theirs set them up on a date and by April 2022, the couple were seen in displaying tons of PDA around New York City.

    More recently, the pair attended a wedding where Wooten was the best man. A source who also attended the wedding was quoted by US Magazine saying, "Whenever they would go without seeing each other, they would kiss and be very happy to be together again. Katie and Bobby were very affectionate with each other at all times; they looked very in love and didn’t care who saw... Katie seemed to be carefree the entire night and very much in love with Bobby."

    She was also said to be “having fun, [she] never tried to steal the spotlight and really let the bride shine”. Unfortunately, this isn't the only time a bride-to-be had to worry about Katie Holmes potentially ruining her wedding with by simply being in the picture.

    Katie Holmes Has Had Her Fair Share Of Trouble In Her Love Life

    Back in 2020, 41-year-old Katie Holmes was seen in New York City getting pretty cozy with 33-year-old chef Emilio Vitolo Jr. The couple were spotted by paparazzi going out to dinner and sharing kisses, which were in the tabloids the following day. The only problem with this is that Emilio Vitolo Jr. was already engaged to someone at the time. Rachel Emmons, a 24-year-old designer, was Emilio's fiancé and live-in girlfriend.

    In an article posted by Micky AU, a source who knew about the situation personally was quoted saying “Rachel was completely blindsided – she received a coldhearted breakup text from Emilio as soon as he knew the photos with Katie were going public. She knows Katie should have been aware that Emilio was engaged.”

    Emilio and Rachel were together for nearly 2 years before their engagement was announced on Instagram in 2019. That same year, in October, another source was quoted by Daily Mail UK saying that that's when Katie and Emilio became "friendly."

    "Katie definitely knew Emilio had a fiancé and started something with him while he was very much living with his fiancé... Up to this press coming out Rachel had no idea what was going on. He is a cheater, and this isn't a 'happy ending' story." Katie and Emilio split up in May 2021, a mere 8 months later.
